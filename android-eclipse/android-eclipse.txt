错误原因：
工程目录下有很多.svn目录

解决方法：
Open properties of project in Eclipse then Resources -> Resource filters.
Click the "Add..." button -> Check "Exclude all", "Files and folders", "All children". In the text entry box input ".svn" (without quotes).
Restart Eclipse.
