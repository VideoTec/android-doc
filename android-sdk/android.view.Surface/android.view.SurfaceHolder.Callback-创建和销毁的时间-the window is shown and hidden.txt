Surface 创建和销毁的时间点：
The Surface will be created for you while the SurfaceView's window is visible; 
you should implement 
surfaceCreated(SurfaceHolder) and 
surfaceDestroyed(SurfaceHolder) to 
discover when the Surface is created and destroyed as the window is shown and hidden.
