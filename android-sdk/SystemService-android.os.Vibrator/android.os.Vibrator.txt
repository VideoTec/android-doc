vibrator[英][vaɪˈbreɪtə(r)] [美][ˈvaɪˌbretɚ]
n.振动器，振荡器，（按摩）颤震器

http://developer.android.com/intl/zh-cn/reference/android/os/Vibrator.html

Class that operates the vibrator on the device.

If your process exits, any vibration you started will stop.

To obtain an instance of the system vibrator, 
call getSystemService(Class) with VIBRATOR_SERVICE as the argument.
